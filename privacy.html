Privacy Policy

Sleipnir Transport Logistics respects your privacy. This page explains what we collect, how we use it, and how to reach us with questions.

Effective date: December 18, 2025

Information we collect

When you contact us by phone, email, or through the quote form, we may collect information such as your name, phone number, email address, move locations, move date, and any details you choose to share.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your quote request and communicate about your move
  • To provide moving and related services you request
  • To follow up on scheduling, logistics, and service details

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only as needed to operate our services (for example, using a form provider to receive submissions) or to comply with legal obligations.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share. However, no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

Your choices

You can request that we update or remove your contact information by calling us.
