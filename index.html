About Sleipnir

Sleipnir Transport Logistics is built around long-distance household moves, with operations centered in Pennsylvania and routes reaching across the contiguous 48 states and Alaska.

The focus is simple: move families and their belongings safely and efficiently, keep people informed, and treat every shipment as if it were our own. No drama, no games, just straight answers and honest work.

What We Offer

Long-Distance Household Moves

State-to-state and cross-country moves for homes and apartments, with proper protection, loading, and secure transport from origin to destination.

Separate, Organized Space

Your household goods are kept in their own clearly identified space with labeling and structure, not loosely mixed in with random freight, so items stay organized and accounted for.

Secure Storage When Needed

If timing doesn’t line up, safe and secure storage options are available, including climate-controlled environments when requested, to protect sensitive items.

Where We Run

  • Primary operational hub in Pennsylvania, serving nearby regions and beyond.
  • Coverage across the entire contiguous 48 United States.
  • Service available to and from Alaska with planned long-distance routing.
  • Straightforward planning for moves that need extra coordination, staging, or storage.
