About Sleipnir

Sleipnir Transport Logistics is built around long-distance household moves, not quick local runs. With operations centered in Pennsylvania and routes reaching across the contiguous 48 states and Alaska, the focus is on moving families and their belongings safely and efficiently.

The approach is straightforward: communicate honestly, handle items with respect, and keep each move as organized and predictable as possible.

What We Offer

Long-Distance Household Moves

State-to-state and cross-country household moves for homes and apartments, with proper padding, loading, and secure transport from origin to destination.

Separate, Organized Space

Your belongings are kept in their own clearly identified space with structured loading and labeling, rather than being treated like random freight, so items stay accounted for.

Secure Storage Options

When timing doesn’t line up, safe and secure storage is available, including climate-controlled environments upon request for items that need extra protection.

Where We Run

  • Primary operational hub in Pennsylvania, serving nearby regions and beyond.
  • Coverage across the entire contiguous 48 United States.
  • Service available to and from Alaska with planned long-distance routing.
  • Support for moves that require staging, storage, or careful timing between locations.
