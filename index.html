About Sleipnir

Sleipnir Transport Logistics is a long-distance moving company based in Pennsylvania and serving all 50 states. Whether it’s a neighboring state, a cross-country move, or anything in between, we prioritize organized loading, secure transport, and steady communication from the first call to final delivery.

Our name comes from Sleipnir, the legendary eight-legged horse of Norse myth — known as the swiftest, most loyal, and most trustworthy steed ever to travel the realms. Sleipnir carried its rider safely across great distances and difficult paths, and we carry that same philosophy into our work: speed without rushing, strength without chaos, and absolute reliability from origin to destination.

The symbol we use, often called the “Viking compass,” represents guidance, protection, and safe passage through any conditions. It reflects our approach to long-distance moving: the route may be long, but the journey should feel steady, planned, and protected.

We’re an owner-operated household goods moving company driven by accountability, care, and hands-on attention. Every move is personally overseen, ensuring your home and belongings are treated with the same respect we give our own.

From the first conversation to final delivery, we focus on clear communication, reliable execution, and protecting what matters most—your home, your time, and your trust.
