About Sleipnir

Sleipnir Transport Logistics is an owner-operated long-distance moving company based in Pennsylvania and serving all 50 states. We specialize in state-to-state and cross-country household moves, focusing on organized loading, secure transport, and steady communication from the first call to final delivery.

Our name comes from Sleipnir, the legendary eight-legged horse of Norse myth — known as the swiftest, most loyal, and most trustworthy steed ever to travel the realms. Sleipnir carried its rider safely across great distances and difficult paths, and we carry that same philosophy into our work: speed without rushing, strength without chaos, and absolute reliability from origin to destination.

The symbol we use, often called the “Viking compass,” represents guidance, protection, and safe passage through any conditions. It reflects our approach to long-distance moving: the route may be long, but the journey should feel steady, planned, and protected.

Rather than operating like a large van line or dispatching random crews, Sleipnir is hands-on and personal. Your belongings are padded, wrapped, labeled, and accounted for so nothing is misplaced or mishandled. You get direct communication and a single point of contact throughout the entire move.

Our home hub is in Pennsylvania, and we regularly serve long-distance moves departing from or arriving to PA, NJ, NY, MD, VA, and the surrounding East Coast. Whether you're relocating out of state, crossing the country, or planning a strategic move with storage needs, Sleipnir brings reliability, honesty, and professional handling to every mile.
